💡 Recovery & Injury Tips

🍔 Glute strain: Hip Thrusts, Step-ups, and Leg Press are safe. Return to heavy deadlifts/squats after 3–4 pain-free weeks at ~50% prior load.

🌒 Sleep: 7–9 hours. Growth hormone peaks during deep sleep.

💧 Hydration: ~35 ml/kg/day. Dehydration cuts strength by up to 20%.

🎯 Progressive overload: +2.5 kg compounds, +1–2.5 kg isolations when you hit the top rep range.

🧭 Creatine monohydrate: 3–5 g/day consistently — most evidence-backed supplement for strength.
