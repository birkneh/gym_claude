💡 Recovery & Injury Tips

🍔 Glute strain: Avoid heavy hip flexion past 90° under load. Hip Thrusts, Step-ups, and Leg Press are safe. Return to heavy deadlifts / squats gradually after 3–4 pain-free weeks, starting at ~50% of prior load.

🌒 Sleep: Aim for 7–9 hours. Growth hormone peaks during deep sleep — this is when muscle is actually built.

💧 Hydration: Target ~35 ml/kg bodyweight per day. Dehydration can reduce strength output by up to 20%.

🎯 Progressive overload: Add 2.5 kg to compound lifts and 1–2.5 kg to isolations each week when you hit the top of your rep range.

🧭 Creatine monohydrate: 3–5 g/day is the most evidence-backed supplement for strength and muscle gain — take it consistently.
